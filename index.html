These Small Hours

Hi, I'm Henry.

Before you continue to read about what I have to share, I would like to thank you for dropping by my page. It means a lot to me. Really really. Without further ado, here is a little about myself.

I was born in Tucson, Arizona to an engineer and an accountant. I grew up in the sheltered arms of Silicon Valley (Cupertino to be exact) in a traditional asian family. I am a current sophomore majoring in computer science & minoring in statistical science at the University of California, Santa Barbara.

I like to read Murakami, Medium, Reddit, New York Times, and American Physical Society Journals. In simpler words, I like hearing about other people’s stories, whatever or whoever it may be about. Growing up, I definitely felt like I've seen and been through a lot. I think my story is special, just as special as anyone else’s. I would love to hear your story and have the privilege of you hearing mine.

My hobbies other than sitting at my computer all day include competitive (and casual) swimming, running, lifting, and taking long walks. When I'm not doing anything quote unquote productive, I like to write. Find me on WordPress.

I also love watching and analyzing movies (Mr. Nobody), watching competitive eating videos (Matt Stonie), drinking milk tea, & playing Kingdom Hearts. As cliche as it might sound, one thing that I always tell myself is that life is an experience. To meet as many people as I can, to go as many places as I can, and to just see the world.


If you want to know more about me, feel free to scroll down or contact me!
