A Little Introduction

Hello there, I'm Henry:
college student & avid kingdom hearts fan residing in Santa Barbara, California

Before you continue to read about what I have to share, I would just like to thank you for dropping by my page. It means a lot to me. Really really. Without further ado, here is a little about myself.

I was born in Tucson, Arizona to an engineer and an accountant. I grew up in the sheltered arms of Silicon Valley (Cupertino to be exact) in a strict asian household. I am majoring in computer science & minoring in statistical science at the University of California, Santa Barbara.

I like to read a lot of novels, news articles, and blogs. In simpler words, I like hearing about other people’s stories, whatever or whoever they may be. My hobbies other than sitting at my computer all day include competitive (and casual) swimming, running, lifting, and taking long walks. Growing up, I definitely felt like I've seen and been through a lot. I think my story is special, just as special as anyone else’s. I would love to hear your story and have the privilege of you hearing mine.

Recently, I decided to acquaint myself more with the field of computer science. I have had some exposure with computer networking, machine learning and web development. I've since interned for a few companies & organizations as well as making a few projects for fun.

When I'm not doing anything quote on quote productive, I like to write. Find me on WordPress.

I also love watching and analyzing movies (Mr. Nobody), watching competitive eating videos (Matt Stonie), drinking milk tea, & playing Kingdom Hearts. As cliche as it might sound, one thing that I always tell myself is that life is an experience. To meet as many people as I can, to go as many places as I can, and to just see the world.


If you want to know more about me, feel free to scroll down or contact me!
